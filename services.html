Services

Five ways we move the needle.

Every engagement is built around a defined outcome — not hours, not decks. We work alongside your leadership, inside your business, until the capability is yours.

01 · Channel

Channel Strategy

Distribution wins categories in India. We design and rebuild channel architectures — distributor selection and economics, retailer expansion, influencer programs (plumbers, painters, contractors, dealers), and the incentive structures that make each layer pull in the same direction. From greenfield network builds to fixing stagnant ones.

Typical outcomes Wider reach with healthier channel economics, faster secondary sales, influencer loyalty that competitors can't buy overnight.
02 · Brand

Brand Building

In building materials, the brand is built in the trade before it's built in the consumer's mind. We craft positioning, portfolio architecture, pricing power, and go-to-market launches grounded in how these categories actually get specified and sold — by the counter salesman, the plumber, the contractor, the architect.

Typical outcomes Premium realisation, differentiated positioning in commoditised categories, launch playbooks that scale.
03 · Leadership

Leadership Coaching

Growth businesses outgrow their leaders' operating rhythms before anyone notices. We coach founders, promoters' next generation, and senior sales & marketing leaders — on decision-making, team building, and the transition from doing to leading. Grounded in 36 years of having sat in those seats.

Typical outcomes Leadership teams that scale with the business, smoother generational transitions, retention of key talent.
04 · Business

Business Advisory

An independent voice with operator credibility — for promoters, leadership teams, boards and investment committees evaluating category entry, channel investments, leadership hires, or growth plans in building materials. We've seen these movies before; we know how they end.

Typical outcomes Sharper capital allocation, faster and better-informed business decisions, honest counsel without internal politics.
05 · Training

Training

Capability that stays after we leave. Structured programs for sales teams, channel partners, and influencer ecosystems — from distributor business reviews to plumber loyalty and certification programs modelled on what built one of India's fastest-growing pipes brands.

Typical outcomes Field teams that execute the strategy, channel partners who grow with you, influencer communities with genuine affinity.
How We Engage

Growth Advisory & Execution Delivery. Embedded. Outcome-defined.

A fixed leadership commitment each month, embedded with your team, against goals we agree upfront. Scope, cadence, and commercials are discussed in conversation — every business is different.
